## Pro features in detail

### Branding

Make LabelUtils feel closer to your label.

- `/brand` opens a form to set your display name, required submit panel caption, and embed color.
- `/brand_info` shows current branding and premium state.
- `/brand_clear` resets branding to defaults.

`/brand` also tries to update the bot's server nickname when permissions allow it.

<Info>
  Discord does not let a shared bot use a different avatar or online status per server. Pro branding applies to server-specific messages, embeds, panels, and the bot's server nickname where permissions allow it. A future [Pro+ tier](/pro-plus) is planned for full white-label identity.
</Info>

### Submission experience

- `/post_panel` posts a branded public submit button panel.
- `/form label:<text> placeholder:<text>` customizes the optional submission question.
- `/templates` sets custom approval and rejection DM templates. Either template can be edited alone.
- `/extras footer_text:<text> logo_url:<url or none> success_message:<text>` customizes footers, thumbnails, and submitter confirmation.
- `/limits cooldown_minutes:<minutes> max_submissions_per_user:<count> duplicate_policy:<policy>` controls cooldowns, caps, and duplicate handling.
- `/routing approved_channel:<channel> rejected_channel:<channel>` routes accepted and rejected updates.

### Review tools

- `/note ticket_id:<id> note:<text>` adds private staff notes.
- `/reviewer ticket_id:<id> reviewer:<member>` assigns a reviewer.
- `/shortlist ticket_id:<id> enabled:<true or false>` adds or removes from the shortlist.
- `/shortlisted` views the shortlist.
- Pro submission cards include quick buttons for Review, Shortlist, Processed, Contacted, and Signed.
- `/priority ticket_id:<id> enabled:<true or false>` marks or unmarks priority.
- `/rate ticket_id:<id> score:<1-10>` stores an internal rating.
- `/reasons reasons:<reason, reason>` saves rejection reasons.

### Reporting

- `/digest channel:<channel>` posts a weekly submission digest and saves the digest channel.
- `/analytics` shows Pro submission analytics.
- `/export` exports submissions to CSV.

### Storage

- `/storage region:<region>` moves managed storage to West US, Europe (UK), or South-East Asia.
- `/setup_db` connects your own Neon database.

<Note>
  When you change storage region or connect a custom database, LabelUtils migrates existing submissions, tickets, branding, and Pro settings before switching. Old managed storage is cleaned up after a successful move.
</Note>

## How to get Pro

```text
/premium
```

Pro access is handled manually:

1. Run `/premium` to see contact and pricing info.
2. Contact the owner and pay.
3. Receive a redeemable coupon code.
4. In your server, run `/redeem code:<your-coupon>`.
5. Check status anytime with `/pro_status`.
